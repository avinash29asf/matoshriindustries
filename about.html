Who We Are

Matoshri Industries is a professional ceramics, metal casting and metallurgy company based in Kolhapur, Maharashtra. We specialize in ceramics & pottery manufacturing, metal casting & forging, metallurgical furnace lining solutions, and expert fettling services.



With a commitment to 100% quality and customer satisfaction, we serve a wide range of industrial clients including foundries, ceramics plants, forging units and metallurgical facilities. Our skilled team delivers reliable, durable and precision-focused solutions.



We are led by Pratik Chougale and Akshay Sankpal, who bring years of industry experience and technical expertise. Whether you need ceramic products, metal castings, furnace linings or fettling work, Matoshri Industries delivers excellence every time.
